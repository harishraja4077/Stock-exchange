Our Historical Milestones

Click on any milestone card below to expand details about that pivotal year in our journey.

1994

Inception & Commercial Launch

Incorporated as a tax-paying corporate entity, pioneering screen-based fully automated stock trading in India. This set a benchmark for regional exchanges and unified national indices.

2000

Launch of Derivatives & Futures Trading

Pioneered index futures trading based on the Nifty 50 Index. This milestone enabled advanced risk management and hedging capabilities for global and domestic fund managers alike.

2008

Currency Derivatives Trading Introduction

Expanded the financial suite by introducing exchange-traded currency futures, facilitating business currency hedges and increasing overall market access.

2015

Co-location & Ultra-Low Latency Trading

Commissioned state-of-the-art co-location servers at our primary data center, offering microsecond order execution speeds and high-frequency algorithms to market makers.

2021

Apex Retail App & API Ecosystem

Released developer APIs for brokerage integrations, allowing neo-brokers and retail investor applications to trade securely on our direct clearing network.

2026

Next-Gen Cloud Trading Engines

Successfully migrated primary match engines to hybrid cloud architecture, boosting trade capacities to 1.2 billion instructions per day with absolute uptime reliability.
